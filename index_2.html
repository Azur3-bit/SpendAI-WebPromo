The Problem

Why Existing Solutions Fail You

People want to manage their finances, but every tool in the market creates as many problems as it solves.

📝

Manual Logging is Tedious

Logging every transaction by hand is time-consuming — most people give up within days of trying.

☁️

Cloud Privacy Risk

Most apps upload your financial data to servers — creating real privacy vulnerabilities and trust issues.

📨

SMS Goes Unnoticed

Bank & UPI SMS alerts are ignored and never organized into useful data. That's money insight going to waste.

🤷

No Intelligent Analysis

Existing apps show numbers but can't answer questions, spot patterns, or change your behavior.

💡

SpendAI solves all four — simultaneously

Automatic SMS parsing, zero cloud dependency, on-device AI intelligence, and behavioral insights. All in one app. All offline.

AI Architecture

On-Device AI —
No Internet Required

Four specialized AI models working in harmony, all running on your phone, all offline after first setup.

Language Model
238 MB
LFM2-350M
GGUF model via llama.cpp. Handles expense categorization, financial chat, and intelligent spending analysis — all on-device.
Speech-to-Text
40 MB
Whisper Tiny ONNX
Voice query transcription for the Finance AI voice mode. Converts your spoken questions to text instantly, offline.
Text-to-Speech
60 MB
Piper Lessac ONNX
Converts AI text responses to natural spoken audio. Gives the Finance AI a real voice that answers your money questions aloud.
Voice Detection
~2 MB
Silero VAD
Detects when you start and stop speaking for a responsive voice UX. Lightweight and always-on, uses negligible resources.

Total On-Device AI Footprint

Downloaded once, cached permanently. Works forever without internet.

Security & Privacy

Privacy by
Architecture,
Not Policy

We don't promise to protect your data. We architect the app so we can't even access it. Your phone is your private vault.

🗄️
Zero Cloud Storage
SQLite database stored only on your device. We have no servers to breach.
🚫
No API Calls
No backend servers. No analytics. No tracking. No third-party data sharing of any kind.
💻
Local Inference
All AI runs via RunAnywhere SDK using llama.cpp and ONNX — 100% offline inference.
✋
User Always in Control
AI suggests, user confirms. No transaction is ever auto-saved without your explicit consent.
🔐
Your Financial Data
Never Leaves Your Device
No exceptions. No backdoors. No analytics. SpendAI is engineered so that privacy is impossible to violate — not just unlikely.
